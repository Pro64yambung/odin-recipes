Banana Shake

Description

A delicious banana shake made with milk, yogurt, lemon juice and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup whipped cream, garnish

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine banana, lemon juice and sugar.
  2. Process until smooth.
  3. Blend in the milk and yogurt.
  4. Pour into glasses, top with a dab of whipped cream and serve.
